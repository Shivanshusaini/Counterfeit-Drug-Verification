About Counterfeit Drug Verification

This project is designed to help people verify whether a medicine is genuine or fake using a unique serial number and QR code. We aim to protect consumers from counterfeit medicines and ensure safety by providing a simple online verification system.

Problem Statement

Counterfeit drugs are a major threat to global health. This system was built to help users instantly verify the authenticity of a medicine using its batch and serial number.

How It Works

  • User scans a QR code or enters a batch/serial number.
  • Our system checks the data from the official drug batch database.
  • The result shows drug name, manufacturer, and production/expiry dates to ensure authenticity.

Technology Stack

Technologies Used: Django (Backend), SQLite (Database), HTML/CSS (Frontend), QR Code Generation.

Developer Info

Future Goals
